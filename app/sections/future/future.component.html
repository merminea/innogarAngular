InnoGar will shape our future

Our solution will shape the streets and change how we think about car storage

Trends around the world

A new variant of each car is almost always larger and wider than its predecessor. In Germany, cars registered since 2000 have become 19 cm longer and 15 cm wider on average.

Where does this lead to?

Some home garages will become unsuitable for the storage of cars. Also, underground parking garages need to be resized, thus, they host less cars! Growing storage issues across the world. A large majority of families have only one garage, if at all!

What are the consequences?

Corrosion, vandalism, permanent de-icing in the winter perod. All these increase amortisation, reduce user comfort and bring about impairment! And in the worst case scenario they lead to theft!

Where are they forced to store them?

In court-yards of houses, gardens, on the street and in other public areas. So, definitely under the open sky and with no protection!
