What does it protect from?
The elevated heat of solar radiation and its harmful UV effect
Cold and icing issues that accompany winter time
Vandalism
Corrosion
In short, all that a conventional garage protects your car from
What makes it innovative?
InnoGar makes it possible to have all these in a place where building a garage is impossible, no space is available, or, building regulation would not permit it.
It is inexpensive and mobile compared to a garage, thus you can take it with you to your next place of residence!
Its epoch-making material use, comfort functions that can be used during its operation, minimum space requirement and its innovative movement mechanism.
When to use it
If there is space for constructing a garage or another building, but the building regulation does not permit it.
If there is space for constructing a garage or another building and the building regulation permits it, but you cannot afford it.
If the building regulation allows it and the necessary material resource is available but you do not have sufficient space.
